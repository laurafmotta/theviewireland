The View in Ireland What We Do

The View in Ireland is a social justice and human rights platform, amplifying the voices of the most disadvantaged and vulnerable women in Irish society - the women who come into contact with the Irish justice system.

We publish a quarterly magazine filled with their art, poems, prose and stories.

Our Core Values

1. Feminism includes all women, not just the ones we like who may look like us.

2. Nothing about us, without us.

3. Equality for women across the criminal justice system including sentencing and sex-based needs.

4. Every woman matters.

We achieve our aims by

1. Encouraging creativity, we help reduce reoffending, ameliorate isolation and mental health.

2. Campaigning, for policy and practice reform to create more effective and humane criminal justice system.

3. Highlighting, individual issues that are the root of community concern

4. Giving Voice to the women who feel overlooked by the system.

5. Calling for the Decarceration, of women not posing a risk to themselves or the community.
