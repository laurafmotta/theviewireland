Welcome To The View!
The View is a feminist, human rights platform by and for women in the Irish justice system.
Tell Me More

Art and creativity...

Through the vehicle of art and story-telling women who have gone through trauma, violence, abuse or oppression can express themselves and reclaim their stories. Art and creativity are acts of mindfulness that not only aid to bring about a mindful state, they have been shown to change the neuroplasticity of the brain.

Rebuilding the future!

Building skills, confidence and financial stability by selling their artwork through The View Collective, women can rebuild their futures with hope and resilience. The artists keep half the proceeds of the sales of the art sold.

The magazine...

The View Magazine is a quarterly publication by and for women in the justice system. Women who are survivors of abuse, domestic violence, brutalisation by the State and its agencies such as care homes, the church or other delegated authorities and women with lived experience of the justice system including women in Direct Provision, women who live in refuge accommodation and women who have been in prison.

Showing their art!

The View is their platform, to exhibit and showcase their art, their poetry and her-stories and to reclaim the narrative and rebuild their futures. By being acclaimed as creators, artists, journalists, story tellers, women can find new confidence and resilience and pathways out of offending behaviour or abusive relationships. The women who have made these limited edition prints are paid 50% of the proceeds from each sale.
